Instructions for the Guess the Number Game

  2. Gameplay: Enter a number and submit your guess.

Achievements & Badges

Throughout the game, you can unlock achievements and badges as you reach milestones. Some examples of achievements you can earn include:

  • Quick Guesser: Guess the number in 5 attempts or less.
  • Persistent Player: Play the game 100 times.
  • Number Master: Guess the number correctly 50 times.

Sound Effects

The game includes sound effects to make it more engaging! You can enable or disable sound effects based on your preferences:

  • Sound On: Hear sound effects when you guess too high, too low, or win!
  • Sound Off: Play the game silently by disabling sound effects in the settings.
